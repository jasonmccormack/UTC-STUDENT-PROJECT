  • Users

  • How do I change my password?

    In order to change your password, there are two ways to do so. The first option being, emailing your admin, as they have the ability to change yor password for you, or you can reset your password through our email function.

  • How do I delete my account?

    In order to delete your account, you must ask your administrator to delete your account. You can contact your administrator and ask for them to remove your account as needed.

  • How do I change my account settings?

    Currently in order to change your account settings, you would need to contact the admin.

  • I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

    If for some reason you are unable to access your account due to a password issue you would be required to use our contact page. Sending us an email with a brief description of the issue would result in you recieving an email giving you instructions on how to reset your password.

    • How do I remove someone from a team?

      In order to remove someone from a team, the admin must go to the User and head to the drop down menu and select a different team or none.

    • How do I move someone into a different team?

      In order to move an individual into another team, you must go to the Users profile, and select a different team using the drop.

    • Can I remove a note?

      As an admin it is possible to remove a note a user has made. The admin must go to the note and simply click delete.

    • What do I do if I come across a bug?

      If you come across a bug, head over to our contact page and send us an email. Please include what the bug is and when it occured.

    • How do I submit a bug I have found?

      If you have found a bug that throws a visual error (comes up with an error message) send us a screen shot of the issue, if the website is not doing as it should email us what it is doing, and what is should do.
